Our Story

Bringing modern Japanese cuisine to Eugene with innovative techniques and fresh, quality ingredients. Our menu features a complete selection including fresh sushi and sashimi, bento boxes, tempura, ramen, and creative rolls.

Whether you're dining in or ordering for delivery, we're committed to providing you with exceptional quality and contemporary dining experiences. Located in downtown Eugene, Sushi Ya is perfect for casual dining, family meals, or group gatherings.
