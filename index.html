Our Story

Bringing authentic Japanese cuisine to Eugene with traditional techniques and fresh, local ingredients. Our menu features a complete selection including fresh sushi and sashimi, bento boxes, tempura, ramen, and more.

Whether you're dining in or ordering for delivery, we're committed to providing you with exceptional quality and warm hospitality. Located in downtown Eugene, Sushi Ya is perfect for casual dining, family meals, or group gatherings.
